## 7 Layers Explained (Security View)
- Layer 1: Physical – cable tapping
- Layer 2: Data Link – ARP spoofing
- Layer 3: Network – IP spoofing
- Layer 4: Transport – TCP/UDP attacks
- Layer 5–7: Session/Presentation/Application – malware, web attacks

## Why OSI Model is Important in Cybersecurity
- Helps identify attack surface
- Helps in troubleshooting
- Used during pentesting & SOC analysis
